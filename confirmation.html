Health Checkup Booked!

Thank you for booking your health checkup with our hospital. We appreciate your trust in our services and look forward to assisting you with your healthcare needs.

Please note the following important information for your health checkup:

1. Arrive at the hospital 15 minutes prior to your scheduled appointment.

2. Bring a valid identification document (e.g., passport, driver's license) for registration purposes.

3. Ensure that you have followed any specific pre-checkup instructions provided by your doctor or mentioned during the booking process.

4. If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, please contact our hospital at least 24 hours in advance.
